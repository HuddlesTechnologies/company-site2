About Huddles Technologies

Huddles Technologies is a forward thinking digital agency brand operating under Huddles Technologies Ltd, a duly registered Limited Liability Company (LLC) in Nigeria, with a growing presence across Africa and Canada. We design and build user focused web and mobile solutions that help businesses grow, scale, and stand out in competitive markets.

We deliver end-to-end digital solutions across web development, mobile app development, branding and visual identity, UI/UX design, product design, and social media management. Our focus is on creating high performance digital products and experiences designed around real user needs and measurable business outcomes. From responsive websites and e-commerce platforms to custom web applications and intuitive mobile experiences, we build cohesive digital ecosystems that are both visually compelling and technically robust.

Our multidisciplinary team of developers, designers, and product strategists combines modern technologies with proven best practices to deliver secure, scalable, and high-impact digital solutions. From concept to deployment, we work closely with our clients to translate ideas into value driven products that enhance engagement, streamline operations, and drive revenue.

Beyond client services, Huddles Technologies develops and operates proprietary digital products, applications, and platforms owned by Huddles Technologies Ltd. This in house product development capability allows us to validate ideas in real market conditions, strengthen our product engineering discipline, and continuously refine how we design and deliver scalable, user-centered solutions.

With a collaborative, client first approach, we partner with businesses at every stage, whether you’re launching a new brand, expanding your digital presence, or building scalable software. Our goal is simple: to design, build, and manage digital products that deliver measurable results and long term value.

With operations in Nigeria 🇳🇬 and Canada 🇨🇦, Huddles Technologies brings a global perspective and a commitment to excellence to every project. Operated by Huddles Technologies Ltd, we don’t just build digital products, we create meaningful digital experiences and proprietary platforms that drive real business impact.

Huddles Technologies Remote Work Policy

Purpose:
Huddles Technologies is committed to creating a flexible and productive work environment that supports remote work while maintaining the highest standards of service, innovation, and collaboration. This policy outlines expectations, responsibilities, and guidelines for all employees working remotely.

Scope
This policy applies to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees of Huddles Technologies Ltd who are approved to work remotely, either temporarily or permanently.

Core Principles

  • Innovation
    • Employees are encouraged to propose new ideas, tools, and workflows to improve efficiency and deliver creative solutions.
    • Remote work should support experimentation and continuous improvement while maintaining accountability.
  • Excellence
    • All team members are expected to maintain high standards of work quality, responsiveness, and professionalism, regardless of work location.
    • Deliverables should meet or exceed client expectations and internal benchmarks.
  • Collaboration
    • Clear, timely communication is essential to ensure all team members remain connected and aligned.
    • Remote employees are expected to actively participate in team meetings, brainstorming sessions, and collaborative platforms.

Work Expectations

  • Working Hours: Employees should adhere to agreed upon working hours and remain available for core collaboration periods.
  • Availability & Communication: Use company approved communication tools (Slack, Teams, email) to stay reachable and responsive.
  • Task Management: All work must be tracked in project management tools (e.g. Trello, Jira, Asana) to ensure transparency.
  • Performance & Deliverables: Remote employees are accountable for meeting deadlines, quality standards, and client satisfaction.

Technology & Security

  • Employees must use company provided devices or secure personal devices with up-to-date antivirus software.
  • All work must comply with Huddles Technologies’ privacy policy, privacy management, and legal guidelines.
  • Confidential information must be handled securely at all times, including during remote access to company systems.

Meetings & Collaboration

  • Remote employees must attend scheduled virtual meetings unless excused.
  • Team check-ins and stand-ups should be prioritized to maintain alignment and foster collaboration.
  • Employees are encouraged to document and share progress, challenges, and ideas to support transparency.

Remote Work Guidelines

  • Maintain a dedicated workspace that allows focus and minimizes distractions.
  • Ensure reliable internet and power access during working hours.
  • Take regular breaks to support health and productivity.

Policy Review & Updates
This policy may be updated periodically to reflect organizational changes, technological advancements, or feedback from employees.

By adhering to this policy, Huddles Technologies employees contribute to a culture of innovation, excellence, and collaboration, whether working in office or remotely, while delivering outstanding results for our clients and partners.
